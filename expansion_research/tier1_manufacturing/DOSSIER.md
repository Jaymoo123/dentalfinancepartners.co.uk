  (skynetaccounting.co.uk, "Accountants For Manufacturing & Engineering Business
  Owners"). No exact-match domains exist. On that narrow reading R2 was right.

  SECTION rivals**, and unlike care's young SEO-led field this one is institutional —
  MHA and Azets (national top-15/top-10) run dedicated manufacturing & engineering
  teams; PKF Francis Clark serves "more than 300 manufacturing and engineering
  businesses"; Hurst has been "specialist advisors to many of the UK's foremost
  manufacturing and engineering businesses... since 1982". The head SERPs are owned by
  real specialists with client books, not by thin sector pages. The true SECTION count
  is higher still: Crowe, Price Bailey, Mitchell Charlesworth were bot-blocked and
  excluded rather than guessed at (COMPETITORS.md).
- **Search intent is weak/contaminated where the moat was claimed.** The hire head
  family is modest — R2D_VOLUMES.md row 22 measured **family 260/mo, head
  "manufacturing accountant" 170/mo** (checker correction: the ~390/mo in
  R2_NICHE_SCORES_FINAL.md's narrative does not match R2D's own table and looks like a
  transcription; the lower measured figure strengthens this verdict). Unre-measured
  this run. Job boards ranked in the
  engineering-accountant queries; and the service long-tail is owned by non-hire
  intent — R&D SERPs by VC-backed boutiques with calculators (ForrestBrown, EmpowerRD,
  randd, TBAT...), costing/WIP/overhead SERPs by software vendors and education sites
  (majority student/DIY intent, measured in TOPICS.md), CBAM by EU-language queries.
- **The estate walls cut the remaining moat.** 49 measured estate dupes + 256
  wall-enforced drops show the generic capital-allowances family already belongs to
  generalist/dentists/property; generic R&D belongs to contractors-ir35 (48 drops).
  Two of the three claimed moat clusters are only half-available (TOPICS.md finding).

Per the promotion rule set in this dossier's R3 task brief (<3 genuine dedicated rivals
AND weak search intent → PARK-candidate — dossier-local framing, not
EXPANSION_PROGRAM.md doctrine): **both conditions hold. Do not build this niche on the current
evidence.** The un-forced case FOR it (fresh FA 2026 CA content where incumbents are
stale, the tool vacuum outside R&D, the unowned CBAM/exporter wedge) is real but is a
wedge, not a field — it cannot carry a site against institutional incumbents on a
measured 260/mo head-family demand. Final call needs only the cheap paid volume pull below; if the head family
re-measures materially above R2D's figure the verdict can be revisited at the owner gate.
