# R3 tier2 FCA-regulated firms: verified competitor landscape

Date: 2026-07-15. Source: 32 DDG buyer-intent queries (uk-en), 206 surviving domains
fetch-verified (raw/verify_evidence.json), 0 estate leaks (hard assert). Free sources only.

## Headline

The buyer-intent SERPs for "accountants for FCA regulated firms", CASS audit, safeguarding
audit, MIFIDPRU/IFPR reporting are NOT owned by accountants. They are owned by three other
verticals: FCA compliance consultancies, financial-services law firms, and regtech software.
Accountancy presence is 2 dedicated microsites plus thin section pages on generalist firms.

## Read

R2's "weak field" holds only for the accountancy slice. The full SERP field is crowded
with well-funded compliance consultancies that answer the same buyer intent and can also
serve the non-audit work (authorisation packs, RegData submissions, ICARA support). A new
faceless content site competes against them, not just against the 2 accountancy microsites.
