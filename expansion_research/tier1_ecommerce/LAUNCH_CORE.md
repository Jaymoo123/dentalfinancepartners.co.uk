Date: 2026-07-12. **No volumes/KD/CPC this run (zero DataForSEO by rule)** — all demand
claims below are precedent/family-size-based and must be re-scored with the paid pulls
(DOSSIER.md "TODO — paid pulls") before build commitment. Competitive read = COMPETITORS.md:
**14 dedicated firms, 47 section firms, and a SaaS/tool adjacent layer that owns most of
the informational SERP.** R2 scored this "hardest verified fight; build last with the most
ambitious asset plan" — this run confirms it.

## Strategic consequence: NARROWER AND DEEPER than every other niche

The care/pharmacies pattern (broad /for/* hub family across sub-segments) would launch us
into 14 dedicated incumbents' strongest pages simultaneously, with A2X/Link My Books
(868-page content arm) above us on every informational query and HMRC's own side-hustle
campaign occupying the DIY end. The honest read:

- **Do NOT contest**: generic "ecommerce accountant" head SERPs at launch (13-year-old
  exact-match incumbents with 100-250-page hubs); pure fee calculators (1,375-URL
  programmatic incumbent); "best ecommerce accounting software" listicles (SaaS-owned);
  generic bookkeeping-how-to (SaaS-owned).
- **Contest**: (1) the UK-tax-specific layer the SaaS arms can't credibly own (they sell
  software, not tax positions), (2) marketplace-collected-VAT and establishment edge cases
  (HOUSE_POSITIONS A), (3) fee+VAT+income-tax JOINED calculators (CALCULATORS.md launch
  tier — verified vacuum), (4) the data asset (verified vacuum, DATA_ASSET.md).
- Launch core is therefore **smaller than care's 22 pages**: 14 pages + 3 tools + 1 asset,
  with depth concentrated on VAT/cross-border.

segment-specific lead-form optional fields (platforms sold on, monthly revenue band, VAT
registration status, stock location UK/EU/China, fulfilment model FBA/FBM/3PL).

## Intent classes

1. **HIRE** (lead pages): "amazon (fba) accountant uk", "ecommerce accountant",
   "shopify accountant", "accountants for amazon sellers" — all verified rival-targeted
   terms; hardest class, treat as long game.
2. **SELLER-PROBLEM** (assist + capture): VAT threshold confusion (gross vs payout),
   deemed-supplier/establishment, VAT on Amazon fees, IOSS/EU after Brexit, settlement
   reconciliation, COGS/"profit higher than bank balance", HMRC platform-report letters.
   Calculator/checker surfaces. **This is the winnable class.**
3. **DIY-INFORMATIONAL** (authority + citations): side-hustle tax family, "do I pay tax
   selling on Vinted/eBay", MTD for sellers, index-asset queries. HMRC and consumer press
   own the head; we target the decision points that convert (letter received, threshold
   approached).

## Launch core (14 pages + 3 tools + 1 asset)

**Hubs (5):** homepage + 4 /for/* pages above.

**Money/service pages (4):**
- Ecommerce VAT compliance (registration, schemes, marketplace layer) — every dedicated
  rival sells it; ours anchors to the /vat/ depth cluster.
- Amazon settlement / Shopify payout reconciliation service — the bookkeeping wedge;
  SaaS-neutral positioning ("with or without A2X/Link My Books") differentiates from the
  SaaS-partner firms.
- Selling into the EU (IOSS/OSS setup + intermediary coordination) — the cross-border VAT
  agencies own prose, no UK accountancy firm owns it end-to-end as a service page.
- HMRC letter about your online sales (platform-reporting response service) — timely,
  advisory-led, fits the lead model; verified query family (taxhelpforhustles campaign
  occupied our SERPs).

**/vat/ depth cluster (5 pages at launch):** deemed supplier & establishment status;
VAT on marketplace fees (reverse charge; the 2024 Amazon UK-billing switch); the £135
import rule; IOSS vs OSS for UK/NI sellers; postponed import VAT + margin scheme for
resellers. Every position pre-cited (HOUSE_POSITIONS A/B, 32/32 citations live).

**Blog clusters at launch (8 posts, 2 per hub):** platform-reporting rules + trading
allowance pair; VAT threshold gross-vs-payout explainer + FRS-is-wrong-for-sellers;
COGS/inventory basics + cash-vs-accruals for stock businesses; sole-trader-vs-Ltd for
sellers (2026/27 dividend rates) + MTD ITSA April 2026 for sellers.

**Tools (3):** seller take-home/true-margin, VAT threshold tracker, ST-vs-Ltd for sellers
(CALCULATORS.md launch tier).

**Asset (1):** UK Online Seller Business Index (DATA_ASSET.md).
