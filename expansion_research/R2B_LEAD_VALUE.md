## Methodology

- Evidence via web search of live UK firm pricing pages, pricing guides, and lead-marketplace pages (URLs cited inline). **No figure appears without a citation.** Where UK data is sparse the row carries a RANGE + LOW confidence, or "none found".
- 89 niches share economics; they are collapsed into **15 fee groups** (below). Each row states its group; niche-specific evidence is added where found.
- **Lead-value score (1-5)** = annual fee × recurrence/stickiness × observed willingness-to-pay for leads. 3 = generic small-business compliance band.
- Known reference point (given): contractor leads sell for £30-80.
