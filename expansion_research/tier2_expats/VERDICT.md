## Verdict: NO-GO as a standalone site. CONDITIONAL as estate extensions.

The R2 "entrenched" prior is confirmed and strengthened, and a second blocker the
prior did not capture is now measured: internal cannibalisation.

1. **Three-layer entrenchment (measured)**: 25 dedicated brands (16 UK cross-border
   incl. institutional Blevins Franks and the 568-URL expertsforexpats.com content
   moat; 9 US-expat-in-UK players whose dual US+UK filing product we cannot offer),
   plus big/mid-tier firms (BDO, Blick Rothenberg, Buzzacott, AAB) owning the
   technical head terms, plus an 11-firm wealth/IFA layer owning pension intent.
   This is the deepest verified field of any niche surveyed to date (care: 12
   dedicated, mostly young; here: 25, several institutional).
2. **Estate collision (new evidence)**: the niche's most attackable commercial wedge,
   non-resident landlord compliance, is already covered by propertytaxpartners.co.uk.
   The dedup gate dropped 10 exact + 29 fuzzy NRL terms as existing estate coverage,
   the property site itself surfaced in this niche's SERPs, and 160 of 1,909 clusters
   are property-adjacent. A new brand here would compete with our own best site.
3. **Lead value 5 not disproven** but not confirmed either (zero CPC data this run).
   High lead value cannot rescue a niche where the winnable turf is this narrow.

## Conditional path (recommended instead of a site 8)

- **Property site**: take the non-resident landlord + NRCGT expansion wave (the 160
  property-adjacent clusters), where it already has authority and existing coverage
  to build from.
- **Generalist site**: take the residence cluster (leaving the UK, split-year, SRT,
  P85, returning to the UK, FIG regime) as a content wave; the FIG-regime freshness
  window and the SRT/split-year calculator vacuum are real, verified gaps that fit
  an existing brand at near-zero brand cost.
- **Do not touch** the US-expat segment (product barrier) or expat wealth/pension
  intent (regulated advice, IFA turf).

Reversal condition: only reconsider a standalone site if paid pulls show the
residence/FIG cluster carries measured volume and CPC well above the care-niche
baseline AND the owner rules out extending property/generalist instead.
