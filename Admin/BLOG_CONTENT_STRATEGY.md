## SEO Strategy

### Internal Linking
Every new post should link to:
- 2-3 existing blog posts (build topical authority)
- 1 service page (conversion path)
- 1 contact page (direct CTA)

### Keyword Targeting
- Primary keyword: 7-10 mentions (natural)
- Secondary keywords: 3-5 mentions each
- LSI keywords: Sprinkle throughout
- Location modifiers: "UK", "London", "Manchester" where relevant

### Content Length
- Tier 1 (high-intent): 1,500-2,000 words
- Tier 2 (problem-solving): 1,200-1,800 words
- Tier 3 (long-tail): 800-1,200 words

### Update Frequency
- Generate 3-5 posts per week initially
- Once you have 30 posts, slow to 2-3 per week
- Update old posts quarterly with new info
