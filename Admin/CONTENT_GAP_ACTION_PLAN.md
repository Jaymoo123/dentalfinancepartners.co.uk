## Content Quality Standards

### Minimum Requirements

- **Length**: 60-150 lines (800-2000 words)
- **Structure**: H2/H3 headings, paragraphs, lists
- **SEO**: Meta title, description, alt text, schema
- **Internal links**: 3-5 links to related posts
- **FAQs**: 2-4 questions with answers
- **CTA**: Lead form mention or link

### Quality Checklist

Before publishing each post:
- [ ] Target keyword in title, H1, meta title
- [ ] Meta description under 160 characters
- [ ] At least 3 H2 sections
- [ ] 2-4 FAQs included
- [ ] 3-5 internal links to related posts
- [ ] Alt text for images (if any)
- [ ] Proper frontmatter (all fields filled)
- [ ] No spelling/grammar errors
- [ ] Accurate UK tax information
- [ ] 2026 date references where relevant
