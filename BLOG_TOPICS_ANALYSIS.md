## CONTENT QUALITY STANDARDS

### Each blog post MUST include:

**Structure:**
- ✅ Clear H1 (includes primary keyword)
- ✅ Meta title (50-60 chars, includes keyword)
- ✅ Meta description (140-160 chars, includes keyword + CTA)
- ✅ Summary (1-2 sentences for cards)
- ✅ 1,500-2,500 words (comprehensive but readable)
- ✅ H2 sections (4-6 main sections)
- ✅ H3 subsections where needed
- ✅ 4 FAQs (question + answer)

**Content:**
- ✅ Real examples with numbers (£80k income, £6k expenses)
- ✅ UK-specific (HMRC, UK GDPR, Companies House)
- ✅ Dental-specific (NHS, UDA, associate agreements)
- ✅ Actionable takeaways (not just theory)
- ✅ Plain English (avoid jargon)
- ✅ Practical tone (not promotional)

**SEO:**
- ✅ Primary keyword 7-10 mentions
- ✅ Secondary keywords 4-5 mentions each
- ✅ Internal links (2-3 per post)
- ✅ External links to authoritative sources (HMRC, NHS, Companies House)
- ✅ Alt text for images (if used)

**Conversion:**
- ✅ Lead form embedded (already implemented in BlogPostRenderer)
- ✅ CTA to book consultation
- ✅ Related articles section
- ✅ Clear next steps
