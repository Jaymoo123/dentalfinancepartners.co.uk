# Keyword Clusters — Agency Founder Finance

_Source: SE Ranking exports (2026-05). UK market unless noted._

---

## Critical framing note

The bulk of search volume in the "agency accounting" export is **recruitment agency noise** — people searching for accountancy staffing firms, temp agencies for accountants, and Canadian Revenue Agency login pages. These are not our audience. The genuine "accounting services for agency founders" cluster is much smaller but has strong commercial intent signals (local pack triggers, £3–6 CPC, low difficulty).

## Cluster 4 — Agency Accountant Specific Services (INFORMATIONAL → COMMERCIAL)

**Theme:** Founders researching specific finance topics for their agency

No data in the exports for these terms — they represent the informational content gap that the blog should fill. Topics include:

- IR35 for agency contractors
- Paying yourself as an agency founder (salary vs dividends)
- VAT for agencies
- R&D tax credits for digital/creative agencies
- Agency cash flow management
- Sole trader vs limited company for agencies
- Corporation tax for agencies
- Management accounts for agencies
- Director's loan account guidance
- Selling an agency — tax implications

These need MCP validation or competitor content analysis. See `mcp-research-log.md`.

**Verdict:** UAE is a secondary audience. The site should reference UAE readiness (non-dom considerations, UK-UAE agency founders) as a brand angle rather than targeting UAE keywords specifically. Volume is too thin for dedicated UAE pages at launch.

## Noise / Excluded Keywords

These appeared in exports but are NOT relevant to our audience:

- All "accountancy recruitment agencies" variants (staffing agencies, temp agencies for accountants)
- Canada Revenue Agency keywords
- "Facebook agency account", "Google Ads agency account", "agency ad account" (platform ad buying, not accounting)
- "Account manager digital agency" / "account executive digital agency" (job titles, not accounting services)
- "Account based marketing agency" (ABM services — different business)
- Travel agency accounting (niche aside — wrong audience)
- Government agency accounting
- Collection agency / debt recovery keywords
- Australian, Canadian, US geographic variants
