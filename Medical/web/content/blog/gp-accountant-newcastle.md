<h2>Services from Newcastle Medical Accountants</h2>

<h3>GP Partnership Accounts</h3>

<p>Newcastle GP partnerships require detailed profit and loss accounts, partnership agreements, and individual partner drawings management. This includes handling profit-sharing ratios, managing practice expenses, and ensuring compliance with partnership taxation rules.</p>

<p>Many Newcastle practices also manage property investments through their partnerships, requiring careful separation of practice and investment income for tax purposes.</p>

<h3>NHS Pension Planning</h3>

<p>The NHS pension scheme remains complex, particularly with the tapered annual allowance affecting GPs with threshold income above £200,000. A specialist <strong>GP accountant in Newcastle</strong> can model different scenarios to optimise pension contributions and reduce potential tax charges.</p>

<p>This is especially relevant for senior partners in larger Newcastle practices who may be approaching or exceeding tapered thresholds.</p>

<h3>Tax Planning and Compliance</h3>

<p>Medical tax planning requires understanding of multiple income streams — GMS, QOF, DES payments, private work, and property investments. Newcastle GPs often have diversified income requiring careful tax planning across different sources.</p>

<p>Corporation tax relief claims for practice expenses, capital allowances on medical equipment, and timing of income recognition all require specialist knowledge.</p>

<h2>Common Newcastle GP Accounting Challenges</h2>

<h3>Partnership Changes and Succession Planning</h3>

<p>Newcastle practices frequently face partner retirements, new joiners, or practice mergers. These changes require careful accounting treatment, including goodwill valuations, property transfers, and pension sharing adjustments.</p>

<h3>Mixed NHS and Private Income</h3>

<p>Many Newcastle GPs supplement NHS income with private work — aesthetic procedures, occupational health services, or insurance medicals. Allocating expenses between NHS and private work requires specialist knowledge for tax efficiency.</p>

<h3>Property and Investment Management</h3>

<p>Newcastle GP practices often own surgery premises or have investment portfolios. Managing these alongside practice income requires careful accounting to optimise tax positions and ensure compliance.</p>

<h2>Technology and Modern Accounting</h2>
