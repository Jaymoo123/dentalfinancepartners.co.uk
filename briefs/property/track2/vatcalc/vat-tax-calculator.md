## Primary intent (the lane this page OWNS)
Target query: **"vat tax calculator"** — a tool/estimation query. The page answers **"how much VAT will I actually PAY?"** (the **net VAT liability**) and explains the TWO ways to compute it:
1. **Standard method**: output VAT charged minus input VAT recovered.
2. **The VAT Flat Rate Scheme (FRS)**: a fixed sector percentage of VAT-inclusive turnover.

**The Flat Rate Scheme is this page's unique differentiator** — neither sibling covers it in depth. Make FRS the centrepiece: how it works, sector percentages, the 16.5% limited cost trader rate, the 1% first-year discount, a worked **standard-vs-FRS comparison**, and when (rarely) it suits a property/landlord business.
