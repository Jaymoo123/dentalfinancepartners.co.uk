- **Council-tax owner liability for HMOs:** cite **SI 1992/551 — "The Council Tax (Liability for Owners) Regulations 1992"** (England and Wales; HMOs are **Class C**). Do NOT cite **SI 1992/612** (that is "The Local Authorities (Calculation of Council Tax Base) Regulations 1992", an unrelated base-calculation instrument). The brief's §30.2/§30.5 references and the "Statute spine" / "Authority links" rows that say "SI 1992/612 / Liability of Owners" are WRONG — use SI 1992/551 instead.

- **2023 change is SETTLED current law, not uncertain.** Cite **SI 2023/1175 — "The Council Tax (Chargeable Dwellings and Liability for Owners) (Amendment) (England) Regulations 2023"**, in force **1 December 2023** (England only). Reg 2 inserts **article 3C into SI 1992/549 (Council Tax (Chargeable Dwellings) Order 1992)**: an HMO "must be treated as a single dwelling" for council tax, using the **Housing Act 2004 s.254** HMO meaning (modified to exclude s.254(1)(e) and (5)). Reg 3 amends SI 1992/551 Class C to align owner-liability with the s.254 meaning. Net effect: an HMO has ONE band and ONE bill, payable by the owner. State this as **settled current law from 1 December 2023, date-stamped, with no announced reversal** — do NOT write "government plans to change this" or "verify current status" (the §30.5 caveat is now resolved). Pre-1-Dec-2023 per-room bandings remain on the list until reviewed under SI 1992/549 art 6.
