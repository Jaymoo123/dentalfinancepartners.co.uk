## Capital allowances ground truth — Finance Act 2026 (c.11), Royal Assent 18 March 2026 — ENACTED, CURRENT LAW
State as current law. NEVER write "Finance Bill 2026", "proposed", "expected", "subject to Royal Assent", or "from next year". FA 2026 is enacted.

- MAIN-POOL (general pool) WDA: 18% → **14%**. FA 2026 **s.28** (substitutes "14%" into CAA 2001 s.56(1)). Effective: chargeable periods beginning **on or after 1 April 2026 (Corporation Tax) / 6 April 2026 (Income Tax)**. A chargeable period that STRADDLES the start date uses a **hybrid (time-apportioned) rate** between 18% and 14% (FA 2026 s.28(2)-(6)). Verified: legislation.gov.uk CAA 2001 s.56 now reads 14%, annotated "substituted ... by Finance Act 2026 (c. 11), s. 28(1)".
- NEW **40% first-year allowance (FYA)** on **main-rate, new and UNUSED plant & machinery**, expenditure incurred **on or after 1 January 2026**. FA 2026 **s.29**. EXCLUDES **cars, second-hand/used assets, and assets for leasing overseas**. Deliberately available to **unincorporated businesses (sole traders, partnerships, individual landlords)** and to leasing, i.e. where full expensing (companies-only) is NOT available. Cite "FA 2026 s.29"; if referencing a consolidated CAA 2001 section, verify the inserted number at legislation.gov.uk at write time, else cite FA 2026 s.29. Do NOT invent a section number.
- SPECIAL RATE POOL WDA: **UNCHANGED at 6%** (CAA 2001 s.104D). GOV.UK measure: "does not change the WDA on the special rate pool which is currently 6%". Any "6% → 4%" claim is FALSE.
- AIA: **£1,000,000 permanent** (CAA 2001 s.51A(5)), made permanent by **F(No.2)A 2023 (c.30) s.8** (RA 11 July 2023) from 1 April 2023. The "temporary £1m until 31 March 2026 / reverts to £200,000" framing is STALE; it is permanent.
- FULL EXPENSING: 100% FYA, **companies only** (CAA 2001 s.45S), new/unused, permanent. NOT for unincorporated landlords. Distinct from the new 40% FYA (s.29), which IS available to unincorporated businesses.
- s.35 DWELLING-HOUSE BAR: no P&M allowances for plant for use in a **dwelling-house** in a property business. Common parts (communal boiler/lift/lighting) and integral features (s.33A) in qualifying non-dwelling areas can still qualify.
- FHL regime ABOLISHED from **6 April 2025 (IT) / 1 April 2025 (CT)** (FA 2025 (c.8) Sch 5). The old FHL capital-allowances route is gone.
- CARS: excluded from AIA (CAA 2001 **s.38B**) AND from the new 40% FYA (CAA 2001 s.46 / FA 2026 s.29). Only car FYA = **100% FYA for new, unused zero-emission cars (0 g/km)** under **CAA 2001 s.45D**, to **31 March 2027 (CT) / 5 April 2027 (IT)**. Cars >0 g/km: **≤50 g/km → main pool (14%)**, **>50 g/km → special rate pool (6%)**.
- SBA: **3%** (from 2%), enacted by **Finance Act 2021 (c.26) Sch 22 para 7(4)**, effective 1 April 2020 (CT) / 6 April 2020 (IT). NOT "Finance Act 2020".
