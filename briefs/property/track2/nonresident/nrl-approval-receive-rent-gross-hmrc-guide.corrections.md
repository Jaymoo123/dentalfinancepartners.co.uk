## HMRC forms (gov.uk-verified taxonomy)
- This page's primary subject = the **NRL1** gross-payment application (individuals). NRL2 = companies, NRL3 = trustees.
- **NRLQ** = the agent's/tenant's **quarterly** return; **NRLY** = **annual** return; **NRL6** = certificate of tax deducted; **NRL4** = agent registration. **Do NOT label NRL2/NRL3 as quarterly returns.**
- **NRL8** = the landlord's repayment claim where too much was withheld — confirm the exact NRL8 route/wording against gov.uk at write time.
- £100/week (=£5,200/year) is the threshold above which a **tenant** (no UK agent) must operate the scheme.
