## NRL scheme statutory basis (the brief's spine is wrong)
- **Cite the live basis: `ITA 2007 ss.971-972` + `SI 1995/2902`**. **DO NOT cite "FA 1995 Sch 23"** (repealed by TIOPA 2010, unrelated). History only: original power ICTA 1988 s.42A, inserted by FA 1995 s.40, rewritten into ITA 2007 from 6 Apr 2007.

## Section 24 finance-cost reducer (individual vs partnership)
- The general **individual** reducer is **ITTOIA 2005 ss.274A-274C** (s.274AA = "Reduction for individuals: calculation"). Headline this for individual non-resident landlords.
- **`ITA 2007 s.399B` is the PROPERTY-PARTNERSHIP analogue** ("Property partnerships: tax reduction for non-deductible loan interest") — only invoke it when discussing property partnerships, NOT as a co-equal anchor for the mainstream individual reducer.

## FA 2026 / 2027 property rates (attribute precisely)
- `Finance Act 2026` = **2026 c.11**, Royal Assent **18 March 2026** (enacted, not a Bill).
- **s.6** inserts the property-rate *framework* (new ITA 2007 ss.6D/11CA/16A/17A) and defers the numbers to Parliament; **s.7** sets the **22/42/47** for 2027-28. Do NOT present 22/42/47 as appearing on the face of s.6.
- **England + NI only**; Scotland/Wales devolved (s.8 / Sch 2). The S24 reducer rises to 22% in step (Sch 1) — no new basic-rate wedge.

## SDLT non-resident surcharge
- The **2% non-resident** SDLT surcharge is **`FA 2003 Sch 9A`** ("Increased rates for non-resident transactions"). **`FA 2003 Sch 4ZA`** is the separate **5% additional-dwellings** surcharge. If the 2% is cited in copy, anchor it to Sch 9A.
