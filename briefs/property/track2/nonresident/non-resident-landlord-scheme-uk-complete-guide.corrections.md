# Statute corrections — non-resident-landlord-scheme-uk-complete-guide

These are manager-adjudicated, source-verified at legislation.gov.uk on 2026-05-30. **They override the brief's statute spine wherever they conflict. Re-verify each at legislation.gov.uk at write time and trust the source.**

## NRL scheme statutory basis (LOAD-BEARING — the brief's spine is wrong)
- **Cite the live basis: `ITA 2007 ss.971-972` + `SI 1995/2902`** (The Taxation of Income from Land (Non-residents) Regulations 1995).
  - ITA 2007 **s.971** = "Income tax due in respect of income of non-resident landlords" (the live regulation-making power, in force to 30 May 2026); **s.972** = "Regulations under section 971".
  - **DO NOT cite "FA 1995 Sch 23"** — that schedule is "Obligations etc. imposed on UK representatives" and was **repealed by TIOPA 2010** (Sch 10 Pt 11). It is unrelated to the NRL scheme.
  - **DO NOT cite "FA 1995 s.42A"** — no such section. History only (do not headline): the original power was **ICTA 1988 s.42A**, inserted by **FA 1995 s.40**, rewritten into ITA 2007 ss.971-972 and repealed in ICTA from 6 Apr 2007. SI 1995/2902's preamble still recites the now-repealed ICTA s.42A; that is the SI's own un-updated text, not the live basis.

## SI 1995/2902 regulation references (the brief mis-attributes these)
- **reg 9(4)** = the letting **agent's** "reasonably satisfied are deductible" test for allowable deductions before withholding. (The brief wrongly cites reg 8 for this.)
- **reg 8** = calculation by the **non-agent direct-payer** (tenant-pays) case only.
- **reg 10** = "Accounting for tax — quarterly returns" (the quarterly-return regulation). The liability to account for unwithheld tax sits in **reg 10(5)**, with HMRC's assessment power in **reg 10(9)**. **Do NOT write "no time limit for unprompted discovery"** — no basis in the text; ordinary TMA 1970 assessment limits apply.

## £1,000 property allowance
- Cite the **ITTOIA 2005 Part 6A property allowance (s.783B series)**, NOT **s.783A** (s.783A = the *trade* allowance, a different relief). Verify the exact property-allowance section at write time.

## HMRC forms (use the correct taxonomy; gov.uk-verified)
- **NRL1** (individuals) / **NRL2** (companies) / **NRL3** (trustees) = gross-payment **applications** by entity type.
- **NRL4** = letting-agent registration with HMRC. **NRLQ** = quarterly return. **NRLY** = annual information return. **NRL6** = annual certificate of tax deducted given to the landlord.
- **Do NOT call NRL2 or NRL3 the "quarterly return"** (that is NRLQ).

## FA 2026 / 2027 property rates (state as enacted)
- `Finance Act 2026` = **2026 c.11** (`/ukpga/2026/11`), Royal Assent **18 March 2026**. (Trap: 2026 c.1 is an unrelated Act — never cite c.1.)
- Property income rates **22% / 42% / 47% from 6 Apr 2027, England + Northern Ireland only** (Scotland/Wales devolved, FA 2026 s.8 / Sch 2). Framework = **s.6**; the numeric rates = **s.7**.
- The Section 24 finance-cost reducer **rises to 22% in step** from 2027/28 via **FA 2026 Sch 1** (amending ITTOIA 2005 ss.274AA/274C) — so a basic-rate landlord gets **no new wedge**; higher/additional wedge unchanged (20pp/25pp). This framing is correct; keep it.
- The individual Section 24 reducer lives in **ITTOIA 2005 ss.272A / 274A-274C**, never ITA 2007.
