> Renters' Rights Act 2025 (2025 c. 26) Royal Assent 27 October 2025. The existing `section-21-abolition-uk-landlord-possession-guide-2026` page is a **headline-rule** page — what s.21 abolition is, when it commences. This page is **operational mechanics** for landlords needing to act under the reformed possession regime: (a) notice templates for the new s.8 reformed-grounds notices under Schedule 1 RRA 2025 (which amends Housing Act 1988 Sch 2); (b) transitional cases — existing fixed-term ASTs straddling the commencement date set by SI 2026/421 and what survives the transition; (c) **evidential pack composition** for each ground — rent-arrears ground (rent statement + dishonoured-DD record + reminder correspondence + statutory-demand-style notice trail); anti-social-behaviour ground (police-incident logs, neighbour witness statements, environmental-health correspondence, mediation refusal evidence); landlord-sale ground (marketing agency instruction, estate-agent listing screen-grab, sale-progression evidence); landlord-occupation ground (statutory declaration of intent, evidence of current accommodation arrangements, dependant evidence where applicable); (d) **post-commencement service-mechanics** — service by post per s.196 LPA 1925, service by email where the tenancy clauses permit, deemed-service mechanics, the practical 4-month / 2-month / 14-day notice periods that vary by ground (extract verbatim from Schedule 1 RRA 2025 as enacted — not from Bill-stage commentary); (e) the **12-month re-letting restriction** on the landlord-sale + landlord-occupation grounds per s.6 RRA 2025 — operational consequences for landlords mid-sale who suffer a chain collapse; (f) tax-side hook only at the end — sale-context CGT treatment cross-ref to A1, BADR availability for landlords whose property qualifies as part of a furnished-holiday-let trade pre-April 2025 phase-out.
