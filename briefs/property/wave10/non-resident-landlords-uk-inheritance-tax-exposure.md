# Working title
Non-Resident Landlords and UK Inheritance Tax: What You Owe and How to Plan

## Statutory anchor

- **IHTA 1984 s.6** — excluded property test (references LTR test from 6 April 2025)
- **IHTA 1984 s.6A** — long-term resident (LTR) test: 10 of preceding 20 UK-resident tax years; tail-period table (3-10 years post-departure per prior-residence count)
- **IHTA 1984 s.6B** — young-person variation
- **IHTA 1984 Sch A1** — enveloped UK residential property look-through (in force 6 April 2017; F(No.2)A 2017)
- **IHTA 1984 s.7** — IHT rate 40% (36% where 10%+ to charity)
- **IHTA 1984 s.18** — spouse exemption (post-FA-2025: LTR-based, not domicile-based)
- **FA 2025 s.44 + Sch 13** — abolition of s.267 deemed domicile, insertion of s.6A LTR test, effective 6 April 2025

**House position references:** §22.X (IHT long-term residence operational depth), §15.6 (non-resident IHT headline), §22.5 (spouse exemption refresh), §22.7 (citation block)

**Do not cite:** IHTA 1984 s.267 (omitted by FA 2025 from 6 April 2025 — historic deemed-domicile architecture GONE).

## Key questions (for Stage 2 research + writer)

1. Does a non-resident landlord who has never been UK-resident face UK IHT on their UK property? (Answer: yes — UK situs property is always within UK IHT per s.6 regardless of LTR status.)
2. What happens when the property is held through an offshore company or trust? Does the Sch A1 look-through apply and how does it interact with the FA 2025 LTR changes?
3. What is the s.6A tail-period table and how should a recently-departed long-term UK resident plan against it? (Need the full 3-10 year table per §22.X.2.)
4. What IHT planning options remain for a non-LTR individual holding UK property directly? (NRB £325k, RNRB if applicable, spousal exemption mechanics under s.18 post-FA-2025, charitable legacy.)
5. Can debt secured on UK property reduce the IHT value? (s.162 IHTA 1984 — but anti-deduction rules for non-recourse/connected-party debt since FA 2013.)
6. What is the ATED interaction (if any) and is it relevant to IHT planning? (ATED is SDLT/annual charge; IHT is separate — note the boundary.)
7. How does the LTR election (s.267ZC) work for a non-LTR spouse receiving a UK property gift, and when is it worth triggering?
8. For non-UK-resident individuals who have never been UK-resident: no FIG/LTR complexity; they are simply within IHT on UK situs only. How does estate-planning look for this group (the "pure" non-resident case)?
9. What is the position for a UK property held via a UK trust with a non-LTR settlor? (s.48ZA trust-side excluded property test.)
10. What are the planning boundaries at Stage 2? (Gifting the property triggers CGT; enveloping attracts ATED/SDLT; outright non-UK holding is only possible for commercial or non-residential assets.)
