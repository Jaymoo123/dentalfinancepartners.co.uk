# Portfolio Landlord Tax Planning: The Strategy Guide

## Statutory anchor

- **Primary:** ITTOIA 2005 Pt 3 (property income for individuals); CTA 2009 Pt 4 (property income for companies); TCGA 1992 Pt I + II (CGT on disposals); IHTA 1984 (IHT on death / lifetime transfers); FA 2003 Sch 15 (partnership SDLT relief); FA 2026 ss.6-7 (property income surcharge 2027/28 enacted).
- **Supporting:** ITA 2007 s.399 (S24 finance-cost reducer); ITTOIA 2005 s.274AA + ITA 2007 s.399B as amended FA 2026 Sch 1 (22% reducer from 2027/28); CTA 2010 ss.18A-18S (CT small profits rate / marginal relief / CIHC); FA 2003 Sch 4A (ATED non-natural-persons SDLT 15%); FA 2003 s.116(7) (six-dwellings rule).
- **House position reference:** §7 (2027/28 property income surcharge 22/42/47% + S24 reducer correction); §21 (Ltd Co + FIC operation, salary/dividend extraction 2026/27, DLA, alphabet shares, CIHC); §21.A (CT three-figure framework); §2 (ATED 2026/27 bands + reliefs); §4 (S24 mechanics + 2027/28 reducer uplift); §1 (SDLT six-dwellings rule; Sch 15 partnership relief); §9 (IHT NRB/RNRB, pension IHT from April 2027, BPR/APR cap).
