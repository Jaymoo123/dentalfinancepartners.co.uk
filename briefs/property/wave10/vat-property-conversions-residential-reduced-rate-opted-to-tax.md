## Statutory anchor

- **Primary:** VATA 1994 s.29A + Sch 7A Group 6 (5% reduced rate, qualifying conversion services supplied on non-residential to residential conversions including changed-number-of-dwellings sub-category) — house_positions §29.1 + §29.7
- **Primary:** VATA 1994 s.29A + Sch 7A Group 7 (5% reduced rate, renovation of qualifying residential premises unoccupied for 2+ years / empty-homes relief) — house_positions §29.1 (Stage 2b correction note: Group 7 = empty-homes relief, NOT changed-number; changed-number is a Group 6 sub-category)
- **Primary:** VATA 1994 s.30 + Sch 8 Group 5 (zero-rate: first grant of a major interest in a dwelling arising from a non-residential conversion) — NEW HP LOCK NEEDED (§29 does not yet extend to the Sch 8 Grp 5 conversion-sale zero-rate mechanics; see F-31 below)
- **Primary:** VATA 1994 Sch 10 (option to tax framework) — house_positions §29.3 (election VAT 1614A, cooling-off para 23, 20-year para 25, automatic dwelling disapplication para 5, recipient certification para 6 VAT 1614D)
- **Primary:** SI 1995/2518 regs 112-116 (Capital Goods Scheme, 10-year adjustment period, £250k threshold) — house_positions §29.5
- **Supplementary:** HMRC Notice 708 (buildings and construction), VATLP (Land and Property manual), Notice 742A (option to tax)
- **DIY Builders Scheme:** VATA 1994 s.35 + SI 1995/2518 reg 201 (DIY housebuilder VAT refund on non-residential conversion to dwelling) — NEW HP LOCK NEEDED (s.35 mechanics not yet in §29; see F-31)
