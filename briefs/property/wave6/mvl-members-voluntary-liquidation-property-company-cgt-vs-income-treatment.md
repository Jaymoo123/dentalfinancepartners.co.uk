> A4 is the **company-exit endpoint** of the extraction sequence: where the founder is winding the SPV down rather than continuing to extract from a live company. Statutory frame: **CTA 2010 s.1030A "Distributions in respect of share capital prior to dissolution of company"** is the pre-MVL striking-off pathway (capped at £25k); **the full MVL pathway** brings distributions out as capital under **TCGA 1992 s.122 "Distribution which is not a new holding within Chapter II"** which deems the capital distribution to be a disposal of the shares. The anti-phoenix anti-avoidance gate sits at **ITTOIA 2005 s.396B "Distributions in a winding up"** (inserted by FA 2016, TAAR with four conditions A-D, treats the distribution as income if the main-purpose-was-tax test is met and the founder engages in similar activity within 2 years).
