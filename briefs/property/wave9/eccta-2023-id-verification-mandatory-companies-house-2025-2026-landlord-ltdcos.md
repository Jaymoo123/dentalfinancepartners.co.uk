(a) **Who needs to verify:** every director of a UK LtdCo property SPV + every PSC holding 25%+ of shares / voting rights / right to appoint directors. Existing directors / PSCs in scope under the phased mandatory rollout; new appointments / new PSCs in scope on incorporation or appointment going forward. (b) **The two routes:** (i) direct verification with Companies House via the GOV.UK One Login route (biometric ID check on passport / driving licence app), or via the in-person Post Office identity document service; (ii) verification via an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP) — an accountant, solicitor, or company-secretarial firm registered with Companies House for ID services. (c) **Multi-SPV implication.** A landlord with 5 BTL SPVs is a director of 5 separate CA 2006 entities, but the same natural-person ID verification covers all of them — one verification per natural person, not per company. (d) **Timing operational anchor.** Once a person is in scope under the mandatory regime, ID verification must be completed before the next CA 2006 filing event that requires it (typically the next confirmation statement or director-appointment filing). Stage 2 verifies the operative commencement state at brief-finalisation time AND mandates write-time re-verification. (e) **Sanctions.** New offences inserted by ECCTA Part 1 Chapter 1 for unverified individuals continuing to act, plus civil financial-penalty regime under CA 2006 + ECCTA penalty provisions; annotation on the public register; disqualification risk. (f) **What the page is NOT:** not a general ACSP business-model explainer (different audience); not RoE-side ID verification (B2 territory under ECTEA 2022 + ECCTA Sch 12).
