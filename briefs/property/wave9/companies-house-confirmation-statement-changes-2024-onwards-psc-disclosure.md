> The LANDLORD-LTDCO ANNUAL-HOUSEKEEPING walkthrough for the post-4-March-2024 confirmation-statement regime, written for directors / PSCs of property SPVs and FICs. Page anchors on **CA 2006 s.853A (confirmation statement)** as amended by **ECCTA 2023 Part 1 Chapter 2** plus the inserted/amended provisions: registered-office "appropriate address" rule under amended **CA 2006 s.86** (no PO boxes; address where documents can be acknowledged); registered-email-address requirement under new **CA 2006 s.88A** (Stage 2 verifies inserted-section number); lawful-purpose statement on incorporation + at every confirmation statement (Stage 2 enumerates the inserted-section provision). All effective 4 March 2024 per the Commencement No. 2 Regulations (Stage 2 enumerates verbatim SI number). (a) **The four things that changed on 4 March 2024.** Registered office must be an "appropriate address". Registered email address must be supplied. Lawful-purpose statement must be made. PSC disclosure tightened (Stage 2 enumerates specifics). (b) **What this means for landlord SPVs using a virtual office or accountant's address.** "Appropriate address" rules in PO-box-only arrangements and pure mail-forwarding services where mail isn't actually acknowledged by a human. Specialist company-formation agents have repositioned services around this rule. Page maps the practical question: is my current registered office still compliant? (c) **The new registered email — who sees it.** CH does NOT publish the registered email on the public register; it's for CH-to-company correspondence only. Page clears the common misconception. (d) **PSC disclosure: what's new + what's coming.** ECCTA reforms tightened PSC information accuracy + introduced ID verification for PSCs as part of the Part 1 Chapter 1 phased rollout (B1 territory). The confirmation statement is the operational moment when PSC information is re-asserted annually; from autumn 2025 / 2026 (phased), PSCs in scope must have completed ID verification before the confirmation statement is filed. (e) **Fees + timing.** Confirmation statement fee from CA 2006 fee schedule (verify operative figures at write time — set by regs); 14-day filing window after the confirmation period; striking-off risk under CA 2006 s.1000 for missed filings. (f) **What the page is NOT:** not a general "what is a confirmation statement" 101 page (well covered by existing competitor inventory + gov.uk); not the ID-verification mechanics walkthrough (B1 territory). Cross-link to B1 (ID verification) as the upstream prerequisite + to `landlord-incorporation-step-by-step-guide-uk` as the incorporation pillar.
