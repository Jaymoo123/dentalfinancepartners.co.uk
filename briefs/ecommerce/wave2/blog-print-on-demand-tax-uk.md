## The wedge (tax slice of a large consumer head)

"Amazon Merch" and "print on demand" together are a ~2,400/mo head, but almost all of it is
consumer/design/"how to start" intent we do NOT serve. We take the thin, high-value tax slice the
head ignores: **how POD income is taxed in the UK.** POD is distinctive because the seller usually
holds NO stock (the platform prints and ships), so the site's signature COGS/inventory story does not
apply and the money question changes shape: is Amazon Merch income a royalty or trading income, how
does the trading allowance apply, and what is the VAT position when you never touch the goods. That
distinctiveness is the wedge; no rival covers POD tax specifically for UK sellers.
