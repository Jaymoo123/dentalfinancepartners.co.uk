## Required structure (H2 skeleton)

1. The short answer: when you must register (BLUF 40-60w: £5,000 income gate; CIOs register at any income; register with the Charity Commission for E&W).
2. Before you apply: what to have ready (governing document, charitable purposes for public benefit, trustees, bank details) — brief BLUF then checklist `<ul>`.
3. Choosing your structure first (60-80w signpost only → link `/guides/set-up-a-charity-cio` and `/guides/cic-complete-guide`; note structure is chosen before you apply because it changes what you register with).
4. **The registration process, step by step** (howToSteps — ordered `<ol>`): 1 confirm eligibility/£5,000 gate; 2 finalise governing document + purposes; 3 appoint trustees; 4 set up the charity's bank account; 5 apply online via the Charity Commission service; 6 receive the registered charity number.
5. After you register: apply for HMRC recognition (BLUF — the separate step founders miss; Commission registration alone does not deliver Gift Aid).
6. Your first-year duties: annual return + accounts + deadline (BLUF; link the annual-return blog and `/services/charity-accounts`).
7. Scotland (OSCR) and Northern Ireland (CCNI) are different regimes (flag, do not detail thresholds).
8. Frequently asked questions.

## House positions touched (figures map)

- **HP 1** (£5,000 registration gate; CIOs register at any income): https://www.gov.uk/guidance/how-to-register-your-charity-cc21b — drives BLUF §1 and howToSteps step 1.
- **HP 2** (annual return: all charities file; tiered at £10,000/£25,000; 10 months from year end): https://www.gov.uk/guidance/prepare-a-charity-annual-return — §6 first-year duties. State "within 10 months of financial year end"; do NOT repeat any "required over £10,000" shorthand.
- **HP 10** (HMRC recognition is separate from Commission registration; needed for Gift Aid): https://www.gov.uk/charities-and-tax — §5.
- **HP 25** (CIO registers with the Commission only; charitable company registers with both Commission and Companies House): https://www.gov.uk/guidance/charity-types-how-to-choose-a-structure — §3 signpost.
- **HP 26** (Scotland = OSCR, different regime; NI = CCNI): https://www.oscr.org.uk/ — §7 flag only.
