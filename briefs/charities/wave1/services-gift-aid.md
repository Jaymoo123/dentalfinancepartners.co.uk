## House positions touched

- HP 10: "Charity tax reliefs require recognition by HMRC... Registration with the Commission alone does not deliver Gift Aid or other reliefs." https://www.gov.uk/charities-and-tax
- HP 14: "A charity or CASC claims 25p for every £1 donated... (£100 gift → £125 gross → £25 claim). Requires a valid declaration, and the donor must have paid at least as much UK income tax or capital gains tax that year as all charities will reclaim on their donations." https://www.gov.uk/claim-gift-aid
- HP 15: declaration must include "the charity's name, the donor's full name and home address, what donations it covers, a statement that the donor wants Gift Aid to apply, and the explanation that the donor must pay at least as much income/capital gains tax as will be claimed. Keep declaration records 6 years from the end of the accounting period; enduring declarations permanently. Claiming without a declaration means repaying the tax." https://www.gov.uk/guidance/gift-aid-declarations-claiming-tax-back-on-donations
- HP 16: "max 25% of the donation for donations up to £100; for donations over £100, £25 plus 5% of the amount above £100. Aggregate benefits from one charity in a tax year are capped at £2,500... Breach and the donation loses Gift Aid." https://www.gov.uk/guidance/gift-aid-what-donations-charities-and-cascs-can-claim-on
- HP 17: GASDS "£30 per donation, £8,000 per year, 10x matching rule, 2-year claim deadline"; "max £2,000 top-up per year"; community-buildings rules: "always flag, never improvise". https://www.gov.uk/claim-gift-aid/small-donations-scheme and https://www.gov.uk/guidance/claiming-a-top-up-payment-on-small-charitable-donations
- HP 18: "Charity shops can operate the retail Gift Aid scheme: the shop sells donated goods as the donor's agent... under the standard and simplified operating methods in HMRC detailed guidance chapter 3 (3.42)." https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/charities-detailed-guidance-notes/chapter-3-gift-aid

Consistency rules: never imply a CIC can claim Gift Aid (HP 22 vs 14); GASDS community buildings: describe and link, never compute.
