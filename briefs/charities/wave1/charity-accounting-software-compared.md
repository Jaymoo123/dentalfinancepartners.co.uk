## House positions touched

- **HP 6 (accounts basis), copied exactly:** "Non-company charities with gross income of £250,000 or less may prepare receipts and payments accounts. Charitable companies, and all charities over £250,000, must prepare accruals accounts." Citation: https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/charity-reporting-and-accounting-the-essentials-november-2016-cc15d and CC31 full guidance (verified 2026-07-11).
- **HP 7 (SORP context), copied exactly:** "Accruals accounts must follow the Charities SORP (FRS 102). SORP 2026 applies to accounting periods starting on or after 1 January 2026." Citation: https://www.charitysorp.org/ (verified 2026-07-11). Date every SORP statement to the accounting period, never "the new rules".
- Consistency rule: do NOT state SORP tier thresholds until the position-7 flag is cleared (open flag 1).
