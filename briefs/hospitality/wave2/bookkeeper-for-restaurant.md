- Tips vs turnover: HP 7 (tronc NIC exemption, not automatic), HP 9 (employer-controlled tips through payroll), HP 8 (Tips Act records). https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/national-insurance-manual/nim02922 ; https://www.gov.uk/tips-at-work

- VAT registration threshold £90,000 rolling (HP 4) if the "when your books trigger registration" point is made. https://www.gov.uk/vat-registration/when-to-register
