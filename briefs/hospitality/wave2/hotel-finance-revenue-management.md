- HP 5: FRS accommodation/hotels rate 10.5% (tradescode governs). https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs7300
- HP 6: TOMS on packaged bought-in travel, VAT on margin only, no input VAT recovery on bought-in elements, applies even if travel is not the main activity. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/tour-operators-margin-scheme-for-vat-notice-7095
- HP 21: Rent-a-Room £7,500 (£3,750 if shared), residence condition; full guest house as trade. https://www.gov.uk/rent-room-in-your-home/the-rent-a-room-scheme
