## House positions touched (docs/hospitality/house_positions.md — ONLY figures source)

- **HP 15 (AWRS), locked:** "Buying alcoholic stock from unapproved wholesalers breaches AWRS due-diligence requirements." Any business buying alcohol for resale from a UK wholesaler must verify the wholesaler is HMRC-approved under AWRS and repeat the check regularly, keeping records as evidence of due diligence. The wholesaler's **Unique Reference Number (URN)** should be verified before purchase and periodically thereafter. Wholesalers trading without approval face penalties. Citation: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/the-alcohol-wholesaler-registration-scheme-awrs
