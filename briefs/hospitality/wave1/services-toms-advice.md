## House positions touched (docs/hospitality/house_positions.md; every figure maps to a position + gov.uk URL)

- HP 6: TOMS applies when a hotel or operator packages bought-in travel elements (accommodation, transport or other travel services bought from third parties and sold as a package). VAT is due only on the margin (price charged minus cost of bought-in elements). Input VAT on the bought-in elements cannot be recovered. TOMS applies even if travel is not the operator's main activity. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/tour-operators-margin-scheme-for-vat-notice-7095

Consistency rules: TOMS (HP 6) applies to any operator packaging bought-in travel elements, not only dedicated tour operators. This is the differentiating framing and must lead the page.
