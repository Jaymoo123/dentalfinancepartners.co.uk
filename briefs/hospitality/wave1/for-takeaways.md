## House positions touched (docs/hospitality/house_positions.md; gov.uk URLs below)

- HP 1: Hot takeaway food standard-rated (20%); most cold takeaway food zero-rated (five hot tests). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/catering-takeaway-food-and-vat-notice-7091
- HP 2: Anything consumed on the premises is catering and standard-rated. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/catering-takeaway-food-and-vat-notice-7091
- HP 3: Zero-rating carve-outs (confectionery, savoury snacks, soft drinks, alcohol always 20%; ice cream standard-rated). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/food-products-and-vat-notice-70114
- HP 4: VAT registration compulsory past £90,000 rolling 12-month turnover; monitor monthly. https://www.gov.uk/vat-registration/when-to-register
- HP 5: FRS catering rate 12.5% (takeaways within catering category); limited-cost-trader 16.5% trap. https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs7300
- HP 20: SBRR 100% at RV up to £12,000, tapering to zero at £15,000; many takeaways qualify and do not claim. https://www.gov.uk/apply-for-business-rate-relief/small-business-rate-relief
- HP 24: MTD for Income Tax for sole-trader operators, self-employment + property income over £50,000 from 6 April 2026. Year-sensitive. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/sign-up-your-client-for-making-tax-digital-for-income-tax
