## House positions touched (docs/hospitality/house_positions.md — ONLY figures source)

- **HP 18 (MGD), locked:** "Gaming machines require Machine Games Duty registration before the machines are made available to play." Any operator whose machines offer **cash prizes** must register for MGD before those machines go live. The duty falls on the **operator holding the qualifying gambling or alcohol licence.** Registration is **not required for machines where prizes are less than the cost to play.** Citation: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/machine-games-duty
