## House positions touched

- **HP 25 (cash basis default), the spine:** cash basis became the default for unincorporated businesses (sole traders and partnerships without corporate partners) from 6 April 2024; accruals is available by election; stock-heavy hospitality typically benefits from accruals because cash basis can distort profit when stock is held at period end; this should be an active decision. Cite https://www.gov.uk/simpler-income-tax-cash-basis (verified 2026-07-12). LOCKED. Consistency rule: default is April 2024 ONLY; pre-April-2024 clients may still be on accruals by default.
