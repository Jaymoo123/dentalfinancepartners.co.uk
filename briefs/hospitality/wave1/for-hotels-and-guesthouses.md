## House positions touched (docs/hospitality/house_positions.md; gov.uk URLs below)

- HP 6: TOMS applies when a hotel/operator packages bought-in travel elements; VAT on the margin only, no input VAT recovery on bought-in elements; applies even if travel is not the main activity. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/tour-operators-margin-scheme-for-vat-notice-7095
- HP 5: FRS hotel/accommodation sector rate 10.5%. https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs7300
- HP 21: Rent-a-Room up to £7,500 (£3,750 if shared) for letting rooms in your own home, incl. B&B and guest houses, but only where it is genuinely the owner's residence; trading as a full guest house is a trade. https://www.gov.uk/rent-room-in-your-home/the-rent-a-room-scheme
- HP 11 / HP 12: NLW 2026 / employer NIC 15%. Year-sensitive. https://www.gov.uk/national-minimum-wage-rates ; https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/rates-and-allowances-national-insurance-contributions/rates-and-allowances-national-insurance-contributions
- HP 7 / HP 8: Tronc NIC exemption / Tips Act 2023. https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/national-insurance-manual/nim02922 ; https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2023/13/contents
- HP 22: AIA £1m; FA 2026 WDA 14% + 40% FYA for refurbishment/fit-out. Year-sensitive. https://www.gov.uk/capital-allowances/annual-investment-allowance

HP GAP FLAG: ADR / RevPAR / occupancy-percentage benchmarks are NOT house positions. Present as illustrative operator KPIs only, never as authoritative figures. Furnished Holiday Lettings (FHL) tax treatment is NOT in house_positions (and the FHL regime was abolished from April 2025) — do NOT assert FHL rules; if serviced-accommodation/holiday-let treatment is referenced, flag for a write-time check, do not invent.

Consistency rules: TOMS applies to any operator packaging bought-in travel, not only tour operators (HP 6 consistency rule). Rent-a-room only where genuinely the owner's residence (HP 21). England default. Tronc NIC exemption not automatic.
