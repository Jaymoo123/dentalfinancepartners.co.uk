## House positions touched

- **HP 22 (AIA and FA 2026 capital allowances), the spine:** AIA covers up to £1,000,000 of qualifying plant and machinery per year (kitchen equipment, fit-out, refrigeration qualify); AIA at £1m has applied from 1 January 2019; from 2026-27 main-pool WDA is 14% (reduced by FA 2026 from 18%) and a 40% FYA is available for qualifying main-pool additions; sequence AIA first, then FYA, then WDA. Cite https://www.gov.uk/capital-allowances/annual-investment-allowance (verified 2026-07-12). FA 2026 detail: FA 2026 (c. 11) s.28 substitutes 14% for 18% (CAA 2001 s.56), effective for chargeable periods from 1 April 2026 (CT) / 6 April 2026 (IT), blended rate for straddling periods; s.29 introduces the 40% FYA. Cite https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2026/11/section/28 and https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/new-first-year-allowance-and-main-rate-of-writing-down-allowances/capital-allowances-new-first-year-allowance-and-reducing-main-rate-writing-down-allowances (both checker-verified 2026-07-12).
