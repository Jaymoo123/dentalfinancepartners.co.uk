## House positions touched (docs/hospitality/house_positions.md; gov.uk URLs below)

- HP 1: Hot takeaway food standard-rated; most cold takeaway food zero-rated (five hot tests; hot drinks are a standard-rated supply). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/catering-takeaway-food-and-vat-notice-7091
- HP 2: Anything consumed on the premises is catering and standard-rated (eat-in seating). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/catering-takeaway-food-and-vat-notice-7091
- HP 3: Zero-rating carve-outs (confectionery, savoury snacks, soft drinks, alcohol; ice cream standard-rated). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/food-products-and-vat-notice-70114
- HP 4: VAT registration compulsory past £90,000 rolling turnover. https://www.gov.uk/vat-registration/when-to-register
- HP 5: FRS catering rate 12.5% (cafes within catering). https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/vat-flat-rate-scheme/frs7300
- HP 20: SBRR 100% at RV up to £12,000, taper to zero at £15,000 (examples 50% at £13,500, 33% at £14,000). https://www.gov.uk/apply-for-business-rate-relief/small-business-rate-relief
- HP 13: Employment Allowance £10,500 offsets employer NIC. Year-sensitive. https://www.gov.uk/claim-employment-allowance
- HP 11 / HP 12: NLW 2026 / employer NIC 15%. Year-sensitive. https://www.gov.uk/national-minimum-wage-rates ; https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/rates-and-allowances-national-insurance-contributions/rates-and-allowances-national-insurance-contributions
- HP 14: Casual/part-time staff almost always employees for PAYE. https://www.gov.uk/employment-status/employee
- HP 24: MTD for Income Tax over £50,000 from 6 April 2026. Year-sensitive. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/sign-up-your-client-for-making-tax-digital-for-income-tax
- HP 25: Food business registration required (relevant on "before opening" content). https://www.gov.uk/food-business-registration

HP GAP FLAG: no HP fixes a coffee-margin or cafe GP% benchmark. Any margin figure is illustrative operator guidance only.

Consistency rules: hot drinks are standard-rated; distinguish eat-in vs takeaway for cold food (HP 1-3). SBRR figures link gov.uk. England default for rates/registration.
