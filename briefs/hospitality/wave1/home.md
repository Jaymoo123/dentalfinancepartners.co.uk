## House positions touched (docs/hospitality/house_positions.md; gov.uk URLs below)

- HP 4: VAT registration compulsory once rolling 12-month taxable turnover exceeds £90,000. https://www.gov.uk/vat-registration/when-to-register
- HP 11: NLW/NMW from 1 April 2026 (£12.71 21+, £10.85 18-20, £8.00 under-18/apprentice). Year-sensitive, re-verify at write. https://www.gov.uk/national-minimum-wage-rates
- HP 12: Employer NIC 15% above the £5,000 secondary threshold. Year-sensitive. https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/rates-and-allowances-national-insurance-contributions/rates-and-allowances-national-insurance-contributions
- HP 7: Tronc paid via an independent tronc is free of employer and employee NIC (PAYE income tax still due). https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/national-insurance-manual/nim02922
- HP 8: Employment (Allocation of Tips) Act 2023, in force 1 Oct 2024. https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2023/13/contents

Consistency rules: England default for licensing/rates/food registration; Scotland and Wales flagged, never silently mixed. Every asserted figure links its gov.uk page. Tronc NIC exemption is not automatic (requires genuine troncmaster independence). Never imply tips count toward NMW/NLW.
