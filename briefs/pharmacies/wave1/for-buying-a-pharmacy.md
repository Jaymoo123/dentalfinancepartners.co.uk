## Hallucination danger zones

- Do not assert a valuation multiple or pence-per-item figure (HP 16); the calculator encodes arithmetic but the page cites no external multiple.
- SDLT on an asset deal is up to 5% on property at non-residential/mixed rates; share deals attract 0.5% stamp duty (HP 12). Do not swap these or apply residential SDLT rates.
- CT relief on goodwill is restricted, not freely available (HP 13); do not imply full relief.
- Capital-allowance figures: AIA £1,000,000 (HP 17); FA 2026 WDA 14% with a 40% FYA, special rate 6% (HP 18). Do not use the old 18% main-rate WDA as current.
- "pharmacy for sale" is broker-listing intent; supporting angle only, do not build the page around ranking for it.
- Ownership and registration mechanics only, nothing clinical (HP 11, positioning wall).
- No credential claims, no named expert. No em-dashes.
