## House positions touched (docs/pharmacies/house_positions.md; gov.uk / NHS URLs below)

- HP 1: VAT-mixed business, zero-rated NHS dispensing vs standard-rated OTC. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/health-professionals-pharmaceutical-products-and-vat-notice-70157

- HP 6: income is contract-driven (reimbursement + remuneration under the CPCF), not till-driven. https://www.england.nhs.uk/community-pharmacy-contractual-framework/ and https://www.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/pharmacies-gp-practices-and-appliance-contractors/drug-tariff
- HP 7: FP34 cycle drives cash flow; monthly submission, payment roughly two months later, advance on account. https://www.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/pharmacies-gp-practices-and-appliance-contractors/submitting-prescriptions
- HP 8: Drug Tariff and Category M clawbacks make gross margin centrally set and retrospectively adjusted; margin-variance analysis is the core monthly job. https://www.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/pharmacies-gp-practices-and-appliance-contractors/drug-tariff
- HP 9: service income (Pharmacy First and similar) is a growing, separately accounted revenue line. https://www.england.nhs.uk/primary-care/pharmacy/pharmacy-services/pharmacy-first/
- HP 25: employer (Class 1 secondary) NIC 15% above a £5,000 secondary threshold from 6 April 2025. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/rates-and-thresholds-for-employers-2026-to-2027
- HP 26: Employment Allowance £10,500 from 6 April 2025 (eligibility conditions apply, single-director bar). https://www.gov.uk/claim-employment-allowance
- HP 27: corporation tax 25% over £250,000, 19% up to £50,000, Marginal Relief between; most single-store pharmacies sit in the marginal band. https://www.gov.uk/corporation-tax-rates
- HP 28: profit extraction salary/dividend mix; dividends taxed 10.75%/35.75%/39.35% for 2026/27, £500 dividend allowance. https://www.gov.uk/tax-on-dividends

Consistency rules: UK tax law default; NHS contract citations are England (devolved variants flagged post-launch). Every figure links its source. Owner/employer frame only.

## Hallucination danger zones

- Employer NIC 15% above £5,000 from 6 April 2025 (HP 25), not 13.8%/£9,100. Employment Allowance £10,500 (HP 26), not £5,000.
- Corporation tax marginal band figures are £50,000 and £250,000; do not misstate (HP 27). For group owners the limits divide by associated companies, route to /for/pharmacy-groups.
- Dividend rates are 10.75%/35.75%/39.35% for 2026/27 (HP 28); do not use pre-FA-2026 rates.
- Do not assert Category M or Drug Tariff price figures; treat as a mechanic, not a quoted number (HP 8).
- FP34 timing is "roughly two months", an advance on account exists; do not overstate precision (HP 7).
- Nothing clinical or patient-safety; ownership and business mechanics only (positioning wall).
- No credential claims, no named expert. No em-dashes.
