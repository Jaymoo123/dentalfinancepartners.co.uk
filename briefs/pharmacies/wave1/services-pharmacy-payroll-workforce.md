## House positions touched (docs/pharmacies/house_positions.md; every figure maps to a position + gov.uk URL)

- HP 25: employer (Class 1 secondary) NIC is 15% above a £5,000 secondary threshold from 6 April 2025 (not the old 13.8% and £9,100); material for a pharmacy's dispenser and counter-staff payroll. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/rates-and-thresholds-for-employers-2026-to-2027
- HP 26: the Employment Allowance is £10,500 from 6 April 2025, offsetting employer secondary NIC for eligible employers; the former £5,000 cap and the restaurant/hospitality-style exclusions were removed; eligibility conditions still apply (for example the single-director-only bar). https://www.gov.uk/claim-employment-allowance

Consistency rules: employer NIC is 15% above £5,000 (HP 25); the old 13.8%/£9,100 is STALE and must NEVER be stated as current. The Employment Allowance is £10,500 (HP 26); the old £5,000 cap is stale. The single-director-only bar (HP 26) is a real eligibility condition, mention it.
