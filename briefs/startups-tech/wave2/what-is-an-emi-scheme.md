## howToSteps (procedural overview block, keep shallow, link deep posts for detail)

1. Confirm the company and the employees qualify (HP12, HP33) → link qualifying-company guide.
2. Agree the option valuation with HMRC Shares and Assets Valuation (HP14) → link valuation guide (NO VAL231 link).
3. Grant the options under an EMI option agreement.
4. Register the scheme and notify the grant by 6 July after the tax-year end, including nil returns later (HP13) → link ERS annual-return walkthrough.
5. Maintain: watch for disqualifying events and file the annual ERS return each 6 July (HP13) → link disqualifying-events guide.
