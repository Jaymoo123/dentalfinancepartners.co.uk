## Hallucination danger zones

- The window is 6 months from the accounting-period END. Do not say 6 months from filing, or 12 months, or tie it to the CT600 deadline. HP3 governs.
- The three-year lapse rule: anyone who has NOT claimed in the prior three years is treated as a first-time claimant for notification. Do not invent exemptions or edge-case carve-outs the HP does not give; flag genuinely unclear cases as "confirm with us".
- Consequence is that the claim is INVALID for that period. Do not soften to "may be delayed" or "may be queried".
- Do not conflate the notification with the AIF (HP4) or the CT600 claim. Keep the three distinct.
- Worked dates are illustrative and must be labelled.
- No pricing, no named experts, no fabricated client examples.
