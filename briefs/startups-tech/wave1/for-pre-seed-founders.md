## House positions touched

- HP 7: SEIS lets a company raise up to £250,000, gross assets no more than £350,000, fewer than 25 FTE, within 3 years of trading. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/venture-capital-schemes-apply-to-use-the-seed-enterprise-investment-scheme
- HP 16: a section 431 election is a 14-day joint election on restricted securities (founder-share trap at formation and rounds). https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/employment-related-securities/ersm30450
- HP 24: pre-trading expenditure is allowable if incurred within 7 years before trade starts, treated as incurred on the first day of trading. https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim46351
- HP 25: trading losses carry forward against future profits; pre-profit startups should still file to bank them. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/corporation-tax-calculating-and-claiming-a-loss
