## House positions touched

- HP13: ERS returns and EMI grant notification due 6 July (payroll/scaling context). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/submit-your-employment-related-securities-ers-return
- HP21: CT 19% up to £50k, 25% at £250k+, marginal relief between; limits divide by associated companies. https://www.gov.uk/corporation-tax-rates (marginal relief: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/corporation-tax-marginal-relief)
- HP22: dividend rates 2026/27 = 10.75 / 35.75 / 39.35%, £500 allowance. https://www.gov.uk/tax-on-dividends
- HP23: employer NIC 15% above £5,000; Employment Allowance up to £10,500, solo-director company excluded. https://www.gov.uk/national-insurance-rates-letters (EA: https://www.gov.uk/claim-employment-allowance)
- HP24: pre-trading expenditure allowable within 7 years before trade starts. https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/business-income-manual/bim46351
- HP25: trading losses carry forward, file to bank them. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/corporation-tax-calculating-and-claiming-a-loss
- HP26: VAT registration mandatory at £90,000 rolling turnover; SaaS place-of-supply may keep overseas B2B revenue out (verify vs Notice 741A at build). https://www.gov.uk/register-for-vat
- HP27 (boundary): IR35/off-payroll stated at boundary only, defer to Contractor Tax Accountants sibling. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/understanding-off-payroll-working-ir35
