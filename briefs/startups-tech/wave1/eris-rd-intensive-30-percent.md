## Hallucination danger zones

- The intensity threshold is 30% and the rates are 86% deduction + 14.5% payable credit exactly (HP2). Do not round, restate, or "update" these. Note the agency site's page references a 27% figure for its own agency context; do NOT import that number here, HP2 governs and is 30%.
- Do NOT invent the precise definition of "total expenditure" or connected-company aggregation mechanics beyond HP2's wording; flag those as areas to confirm with us rather than stating a rule the HP does not give.
- ERIS requires the company to be loss-making. Do not imply a profitable company can use it.
- Worked-example figures are illustrative and must be labelled so.
- No pricing, no named experts, no fabricated claim counts.
- Do not present the 86% as cash; it is an additional deduction feeding the payable-credit computation for a loss-maker.
