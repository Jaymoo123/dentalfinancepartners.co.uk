## House positions touched

The homepage names capabilities, not figures. Where any figure appears it must carry its HP + gov.uk URL; prefer to route the figure to the money page rather than restate it here.
- HP 1: merged R&D scheme, 20% above-the-line taxable credit. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/corporation-tax-research-and-development-tax-relief-for-large-companies
- HP 6: qualifying R&D must seek an advance in science or technology; routine development is not R&D (the honesty moat, use as the "why a specialist" proof point). https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/corporate-intangibles-research-and-development-manual/cird100000
- HP 7: SEIS raise up to £250,000, gross assets no more than £350,000, fewer than 25 FTE, within 3 years of trading (name only; depth on the service page). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/venture-capital-schemes-apply-to-use-the-seed-enterprise-investment-scheme
- HP 12: EMI up to £250,000 unexercised value per employee, £3m per company (name only; depth on the service page). https://www.gov.uk/tax-employee-share-schemes/enterprise-management-incentives-emis
- HP 27: IR35/off-payroll boundary; defer contractor depth to the sibling site. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/understanding-off-payroll-working-ir35
