## House positions touched

- HP 1: merged R&D scheme, 20% above-the-line taxable credit. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/corporation-tax-research-and-development-tax-relief-for-large-companies
- HP 2: loss-making R&D-intensive SMEs keep the SME route under ERIS (86% deduction, 14.5% payable credit, R&D spend at least 30% of total expenditure). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/corporation-tax-research-and-development-tax-relief-for-small-and-medium-sized-enterprises
- HP 3: first-time claimants must notify HMRC within 6 months of the period end. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/tell-hmrc-that-youre-planning-to-claim-research-and-development-rd-tax-relief
- HP 4: the Additional Information Form is mandatory before the CT600 claim. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/submit-detailed-information-before-you-claim-research-and-development-rd-tax-relief
- HP 6: qualifying R&D must seek an advance in science or technology; routine development is not R&D (the honesty moat, lead this hub with it). https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/corporate-intangibles-research-and-development-manual/cird100000
- HP 12: EMI qualifying-company rules (retaining engineers with options; name only, depth on service page). https://www.gov.uk/tax-employee-share-schemes/enterprise-management-incentives-emis
- HP 26: VAT registration at £90,000 rolling turnover; place-of-supply for overseas B2B dev services; verify vs Notice 741A at build. https://www.gov.uk/register-for-vat

## Hallucination danger zones

- Lead with the honest boundary (HP6): do NOT imply that all software development qualifies for R&D. Client-funded routine delivery generally does not qualify; frame as general guidance and route facts to "speak to us".
- ERIS needs loss-making AND at least 30% R&D-intensity (HP2); do not present as a default.
- Project-accounting / work-in-progress narrative is principles-based and has NO figure-bearing HP; do not invent a standard reference, rate or WIP rule. FLAG for Stage 2 if a specific standard treatment is wanted.

- VAT threshold £90,000 rolling turnover (HP26); place-of-supply "may" apply, verify per supply vs Notice 741A.
- No IR35 depth (HP27, this audience overlaps contractor territory: keep firmly to company-side software firms, one-line referral for solo contractors), no agency audience (HP28), no named experts, no pricing.
