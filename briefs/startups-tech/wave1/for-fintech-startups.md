- HP 9: EIS raise up to £5m/year and £12m lifetime (higher for knowledge-intensive companies). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/venture-capital-schemes-apply-for-the-enterprise-investment-scheme
- HP 11: advance assurance is HMRC pre-clearance for an SEIS, EIS or VCT round (highest-intent query). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/venture-capital-schemes-apply-for-advance-assurance
- HP 12: EMI up to £250,000 unexercised value per employee, £3m per company; gross assets no more than £30m, fewer than 250 FTE, no excluded activities, working-time test (excluded-activities test relevant for some fintech models). https://www.gov.uk/tax-employee-share-schemes/enterprise-management-incentives-emis
- HP 21: corporation tax 19% up to £50,000 and 25% at £250,000+, marginal relief between; limits divided by associated companies. https://www.gov.uk/corporation-tax-rates
- HP 26: VAT registration at £90,000 rolling turnover; place-of-supply for cross-border digital services; verify vs Notice 741A at build. https://www.gov.uk/register-for-vat

## Hallucination danger zones

- SCOPE IS THE HEADLINE RISK. Never drift into investment advice, price views, financial promotions or soliciting investment (HP intro). Do not describe SEIS/EIS as a good investment; describe only the company-side eligibility to offer relief-qualifying shares. Do not advise on FCA authorisation or regulatory permissions; that is not tax scope, route out.
- EMI excluded-activities test: flag that some financial-services activities can affect EMI qualification, but do NOT assert a specific fintech sub-sector qualifies or is excluded; frame as verify-per-company (HP12), route to "speak to us".
- SEIS is company raise up to £250,000 (HP7); EIS up to £5m/year, £12m lifetime, higher for KIC (HP9). Do not swap or misstate the two.
- R&D honesty moat (HP6): routine financial-product build does not automatically qualify.
- VAT place-of-supply "may" apply to cross-border financial-services software; verify per supply vs Notice 741A (HP26); do not state a blanket treatment.
- No IR35 depth (HP27), no agency audience (HP28), no named experts, no pricing.
