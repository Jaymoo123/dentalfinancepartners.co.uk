## House positions touched

- HP1: merged R&D scheme = 20% above-the-line taxable expenditure credit, periods beginning on/after 1 Apr 2024. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/corporation-tax-research-and-development-tax-relief-for-large-companies
- HP2: ERIS = 86% additional deduction + 14.5% payable credit for loss-making SMEs with R&D spend at least 30% of total expenditure. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/corporation-tax-research-and-development-tax-relief-for-small-and-medium-sized-enterprises
- HP3: claim notification within 6 months of period end for first-time (or lapsed 3-year) claimants. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/tell-hmrc-that-youre-planning-to-claim-research-and-development-rd-tax-relief
- HP4: Additional Information Form mandatory before the CT600 claim. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/submit-detailed-information-before-you-claim-research-and-development-rd-tax-relief
- HP5: advance assurance for small first-time claimants (trust anchor, not blanket recommendation). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/research-and-development-tax-relief-advance-assurance
- HP6: qualifying R&D must seek an advance in science or technology; routine development does not qualify. https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/corporate-intangibles-research-and-development-manual/cird100000 (hub: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/corporation-tax-research-and-development-rd-relief)
- HP27 (boundary): IR35/off-payroll stated at boundary only, defer to Contractor Tax Accountants sibling. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/understanding-off-payroll-working-ir35
