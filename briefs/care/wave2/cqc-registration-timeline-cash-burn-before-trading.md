2. ## House positions touched

- HP 21: registration mandatory before regulated activity; trading before it is a criminal offence. https://www.cqc.org.uk/guidance-providers/registration
- HP 22 (link only): FVS on CQC's template. https://www.cqc.org.uk/guidance-regulation/providers/registration/supporting-documents-provider/document/financial-viability-template
