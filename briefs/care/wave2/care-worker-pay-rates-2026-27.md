## House positions touched

- HP 7: NLW/NMW from 1 April 2026, £12.71 (21+), £10.85 (18 to 20), £8.00 (under-18/apprentice). https://www.gov.uk/national-minimum-wage-rates
- HP 11: employer NIC 15% above £5,000 (£96/week). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/rates-and-thresholds-for-employers-2025-to-2026
- HP 12: Employment Allowance up to £10,500. https://www.gov.uk/claim-employment-allowance
- HP 10: holiday accrual 12.07% for irregular-hours staff. https://www.gov.uk/holiday-entitlement-rights
- HP 6: travel-time between calls is working time for NMW. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/calculating-the-minimum-wage/working-hours-for-which-the-minimum-wage-must-be-paid
- HP 5: sleep-in NMW, awake-for-work only (Mencap). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/calculating-the-minimum-wage/working-hours-for-which-the-minimum-wage-must-be-paid
- HP 8: AMAP mileage 55p first 10,000 miles from 6 April 2026. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/rates-and-thresholds-for-employers-2026-to-2027
