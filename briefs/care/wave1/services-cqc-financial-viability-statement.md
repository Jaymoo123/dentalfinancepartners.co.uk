## House positions touched

- HP 22: "New providers must submit a financial viability statement to CQC, normally prepared or signed by an accountant" on CQC's own template. https://www.cqc.org.uk/guidance-regulation/providers/registration/supporting-documents-provider/document/financial-viability-template

- HP 23: CQC market oversight financial-distress monitoring (Care Act 2014) for large providers, sector-risk narrative only. https://www.cqc.org.uk/guidance-providers/market-oversight-corporate-providers/market-oversight-adult-social-care
