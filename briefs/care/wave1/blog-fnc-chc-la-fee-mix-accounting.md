## Required structure

BLUF rule: every money/guide H2 opens with a citable 40-60 word answer first.

H2 skeleton:
1. How much is NHS-funded nursing care? (BLUF: FNC is a flat weekly NHS payment to nursing homes for the nursing element of care; from 1 April 2026 the standard rate is £267.68 per week and the higher rate is £368.24, paid to the home directly regardless of the home's own fee level) [HP24]
2. FNC is not CHC: why the difference changes everything (BLUF: FNC is a nursing top-up on top of the resident's own fees; NHS continuing healthcare funds the whole package and makes the NHS the customer, which changes both the fee framework and the VAT analysis; never conflate the two) [HP24, HP25]
3. Accounting for a mixed income base: FNC vs LA vs self-funder vs CHC (BLUF + worked table showing each stream recognised separately; FNC income accounted for separately from LA and self-funder fee income; why blending distorts occupancy and margin reporting) [HP24, HP25]
4. What each payer actually pays and the cross-subsidy problem (self-funders often subsidise below-cost LA placements; show the per-bed-week economics)
5. The local authority fee-negotiation lever: fair cost of care (BLUF: LAs have a statutory duty under Care Act 2014 guidance to pay a fee reflecting the actual cost of care; providers can challenge below-cost rates using the Care Act statutory guidance, the market sustainability regime and the LA's own cost-of-care assessment data) [HP26]
6. Building the cost-of-care evidence pack (how to translate your own loaded cost per bed-week, staffing, occupancy and voids into an evidence-based fee submission; what an LA expects to see)
7. Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland: different regimes (flag that FNC/nursing-payment mechanics and rates differ; do not apply the England £267.68 rate outside England) [consistency: default England]
8. Getting your fee-mix model right (route to /for/care-homes + the funded-nursing-care fee-mix calculator) and next-step CTA

## House positions touched

- HP24 (FNC flat weekly NHS payment: £267.68 standard, £368.24 higher, from 1 April 2026; prior standard £254.06; account for FNC separately). https://www.gov.uk/government/news/better-community-care-thanks-to-nursing-funding-boost
- HP25 (CHC funds the full package; supply is to the NHS; alters VAT and fee analysis; do not conflate FNC with CHC). https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/national-framework-for-nhs-continuing-healthcare-and-nhs-funded-nursing-care
- HP26 (LA statutory duty to pay a fee reflecting actual cost of care; Care Act 2014 statutory guidance + market sustainability regime as the negotiation framework). https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/care-act-statutory-guidance/care-and-support-statutory-guidance ; https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/market-sustainability-and-improvement-fund-2024-to-2025
