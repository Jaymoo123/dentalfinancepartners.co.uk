## House positions touched

- HP 21: CQC registration mandatory before regulated activities; application includes a financial leg; trading before registration is a criminal offence. https://www.cqc.org.uk/guidance-providers/registration
- HP 22: new providers submit a CQC financial viability statement on CQC's template, normally prepared/signed by an accountant. https://www.cqc.org.uk/guidance-regulation/providers/registration/supporting-documents-provider/document/financial-viability-template
- HP 23: CQC market oversight for the largest providers (sector-risk narrative context only). https://www.cqc.org.uk/guidance-providers/market-oversight-corporate-providers/market-oversight-adult-social-care
- HP 18: CT 19%/25% + marginal relief (structure choice). https://www.gov.uk/corporation-tax-rates
- HP 28: dividends 10.75/35.75/39.35% + £500 allowance (owner extraction). https://www.gov.uk/tax-on-dividends
- HP 27: MTD-IT £50,000 threshold from 6 April 2026 (owner-operators held personally). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/check-if-youre-eligible-for-making-tax-digital-for-income-tax
- HP 11, HP 12: employer NIC 15% above £5,000; Employment Allowance up to £10,500 (first-year payroll). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/rates-and-thresholds-for-employers-2025-to-2026 ; https://www.gov.uk/claim-employment-allowance
- HP 1: VAT welfare exemption position from the date of registration approval (first-year VAT set-up). https://www.gov.uk/guidance/welfare-services-and-goods-notice-7012
