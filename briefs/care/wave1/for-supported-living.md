- HP 2: RCB 2/2025 HMRC challenge to VAT-grouping structures using unregulated entities. https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/revenue-and-customs-brief-2-2025-the-use-of-vat-grouping-within-the-care-industry/use-of-vat-grouping-within-the-care-industry
