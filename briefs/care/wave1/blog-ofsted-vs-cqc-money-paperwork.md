## House positions touched

- HP 21: CQC registration mandatory before regulated activity; application includes a financial leg. https://www.cqc.org.uk/guidance-providers/registration
- HP 22: new CQC providers must submit a financial viability statement on CQC's template, normally accountant-prepared. https://www.cqc.org.uk/guidance-regulation/providers/registration/supporting-documents-provider/document/financial-viability-template
- HP 23: CQC market oversight / financial-distress monitoring for large providers (Care Act 2014). https://www.cqc.org.uk/guidance-providers/market-oversight-corporate-providers/market-oversight-adult-social-care
- HP 11: Employer NIC 15% above £5,000 secondary threshold. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/rates-and-thresholds-for-employers-2025-to-2026
- HP 12: Employment Allowance £10,500. https://www.gov.uk/claim-employment-allowance
- HP 7: NLW £12.71 (21+) from 1 April 2026. https://www.gov.uk/national-minimum-wage-rates
- HP 9: self-employed carer misclassification risk. https://www.gov.uk/employment-status/employee
