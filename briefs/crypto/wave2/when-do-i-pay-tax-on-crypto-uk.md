## Required structure (RAW HTML)

H2 skeleton (every H2 opens with a BLUF answer box):
1. The short answer: you pay when you DISPOSE or when you RECEIVE earned tokens, not when you buy or hold (BLUF; cited)
2. When a Capital Gains tax point occurs: sell, swap, spend, gift (except to a spouse) (compact trigger list; link swaps blog)
3. When an income tax point occurs: mining, staking, airdrops-for-something, salary in tokens (receipt value; link staking/mining spokes)
4. When you do NOT pay: buying, holding, moving between your own wallets, spouse transfers (no-gain/no-loss)
5. The deadlines that matter: register for SA by 5 October, file and pay by 31 January (the deadline calendar table)
6. When you must report even though no tax is due (proceeds over the reporting threshold; describe, do not invent the number)
7. When to get help: CARF makes late-and-quiet the wrong bet (two dates + link CARF blog + disclosure)
