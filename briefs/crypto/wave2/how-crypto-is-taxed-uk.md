## House positions touched (docs/crypto/house_positions.md, ONLY figures source)

- **HP 1** (what is a disposal): sell/swap/spend/gift-except-spouse are CGT disposals; most holders are investors. https://www.gov.uk/guidance/check-if-you-need-to-pay-tax-when-you-sell-cryptoassets
- **HP 2** (CGT rate): 18% within remaining basic band, 24% above; higher/additional 24% whole gain; band ceiling £37,700; NEVER flat 18%. https://www.gov.uk/capital-gains-tax/rates

- **HP 14** (many DeFi deposits are disposals · HMRC's current view, not settled law). https://www.gov.uk/hmrc-internal-manuals/cryptoassets-manual/crypto61000
