# UK Agency Formation Index

A sourced, monthly read on new agency company formations across the UK, drawn from Companies House public records, covering 7 agency SIC codes from advertising to IT consultancy.

New advertising agencies (SIC 73110) grew from 3,694 in 2016 to 14,645 in 2025, a rise of 296.5%. In the 12 months to April 2026, 78,419 UK agency-cluster companies were incorporated across all 7 SIC codes, of which 15,067 were advertising agencies specifically. The all-agency union rose 104.2% over the decade, from 36,090 to 73,680 annually.

Software and IT consultancy (SIC 62012 and 62020 combined) is the larger half of the agency-cluster union by volume; the creative and communications cluster (advertising, media representation, PR, design and market research) is smaller but has grown faster in percentage terms.

Formations peaked in October 2025 at 1,427 advertising agencies in a single month, the highest on record.

Source: Companies House Advanced Search API, Open Government Licence v3.0. Full data, methodology, monthly trend, SIC breakdown, creative-vs-tech split, formation seasonality and CSV download: https://www.agencyfounderfinance.co.uk/research/uk-agency-formation-index
