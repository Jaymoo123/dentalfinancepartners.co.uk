# UK Agency Insolvency Index

A sourced, monthly read on agency company insolvencies across the UK, isolated to the exact 7-code agency SIC cluster (not a broader section approximation): 73110 (advertising agencies), 73120 (media representation), 70210 (PR and communications), 74100 (specialised design), 73200 (market research), 62012 (business and domestic software development) and 62020 (IT consultancy).

UK agency insolvencies rose 83.6% between 2016 (550) and 2025 (1,010), the highest annual total in this series. In the trailing 12 months, 965 agency-cluster companies entered insolvency, 3.9% of all 24,768 SIC-classified company insolvencies captured in the same window across England, Wales and Scotland.

IT consultancy (SIC 62020) is the single largest contributor, accounting for 38.4% of the whole agency cluster in the trailing 12 months (371 of 965). Market research (SIC 73200) is consistently the smallest. Creditors Voluntary Liquidation (CVL) is overwhelmingly the dominant procedure across the cluster.

The record-level Insolvency Service file carries a 5-digit SIC field, so this index is an exact match to the same SIC codes used in the UK Agency Formation Index, not a division- or section-level approximation, the finer cut most sector insolvency research settles for.

Source: The Insolvency Service, Company Insolvency Statistics (record-level data), Open Government Licence v3.0. Full data, methodology, procedure and section-context breakdown and CSV download: https://www.agencyfounderfinance.co.uk/research/uk-agency-insolvency-index
