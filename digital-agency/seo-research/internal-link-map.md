## Anchor Text Rules

- Anchor text must be descriptive and relevant (not "click here" or "read more")
- Do not use exact-match keyword anchors on every link — vary naturally
- Max 3 internal links per 1,000 words in body content
- Navigation links do not count toward this limit
- Related posts section does not count toward this limit
