**For:** the link builder. **Site:** https://www.propertytaxpartners.co.uk (use the **www** version — it's canonical).
**Data basis:** Google Search Console (to 2026-06-23) + Bing Webmaster (to 2026-06-25), 28-day window.

---

## Allocation: 4 to the homepage, 13 to commercial pages
- **No local city pages** (Leeds, Leicester, Derby, etc.) — deliberately excluded. The one exception is **property-tax-accountant-london** (London is the biggest market, 263 impressions, and worth the link).
- **4 links → the homepage** for domain-level authority. This is a young domain whose #1 bottleneck is authority, not content. Lifting the root lifts every page.
  - The homepage is being rewritten soon. **Link now anyway** — the URL (`/`) doesn't change in a rewrite, so the equity persists and compounds, and becomes more valuable once the page is upgraded. Don't wait.
- **13 links → one each** on the commercial pages below.

## The one rule that drives the list
Every link goes to a **commercial-intent** page (someone who'd hire an accountant) — never an informational page. The tell: several high-value pages already rank **top-5 on Bing** (proof the content is strong) but are buried on Google purely for lack of authority. Those are the highest-ROI targets.

## Where NOT to point links (would be wasted)
- **Zero-click "rates" pages** — CGT rates, the £3,000 CGT allowance, Scottish LBTT bands/ADS, Welsh LTT, CGT reporting deadlines. They already rank Google pos 3-7 but earn ~0 clicks because Google answers them in-SERP with an AI Overview. A backlink can't out-rank an AI Overview.
- **Local city pages** (per owner instruction) — except London.
- **Off-mission / lookup pages** — VAT "how to calculate VAT for a painters business" calculators, council tax discount, case-law/HMRC-manual lookups.

## Anchor-text rules (important for a young domain)
- **Rotate anchors** — mix branded ("Property Tax Partners"), naked URL, and partial-match topical phrases. Per-page suggestions are in the CSV.
- **Do NOT** place many identical exact-match commercial anchors (e.g. 10× "property accountant london"). On a young domain that's an over-optimisation/penalty pattern.
- For the homepage's 4 links, lean **brand + naked URL** (e.g. "Property Tax Partners", "propertytaxpartners.co.uk").
- Placements should be on genuinely relevant UK property / landlord / tax / finance sites, in editorial context.

## Dial (easy to adjust)
The homepage/commercial split is a lever:
- More aggressive on domain authority → bump homepage to **5-6** and drop the two most-buried (#13 how-to-transfer, #14 spv).
- More aggressive on in-month commercial ROI → drop homepage to **2-3** and add back the next reserves.
