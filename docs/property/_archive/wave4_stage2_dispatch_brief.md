# Wave 4 Stage 2 dispatch brief

**Stage 1 (selection + skeleton scaffolding) complete 2026-05-23.** This doc gives Stage 2 sub-agents the per-bucket guidance needed to populate §4.3 (competitor URLs) and §4.7 (authority links) in each of the 30 Wave 4 briefs at `briefs/property/wave4/<slug>.md`. Stage 2 is parallel per bucket.

## What Stage 2 does NOT do

- DOES NOT change the framing differentiator (§4.2). That's locked by Stage 1 + manager review. Stage 2 may deepen the draft to 2-4 sentences (manager pre-fill is 1-2 sentences) but cannot redirect the framing.
- DOES NOT change the slug. Stage 2 may flag a slug concern; manager arbitrates.
- DOES NOT change the closest-existing pages (§4.5). Stage 1 has already computed the top-5 by Jaccard. Stage 2 may add semantic neighbours that Jaccard missed, but the existing list stands.
- DOES NOT write content. Stage 2 produces the brief, not the page.

## What Stage 2 does

1. **§4.3 Competitor URLs:** 3-5 URLs per page from the v2 universe actionable working set (`docs/property/competitor_universe_v2.md`, 58 domains with ≥2 SERP appearances). The Stage 1 seed URL is in the brief; Stage 2 verifies it's still live + adds 2-4 siblings.
2. **§4.7 Authority links:** 6-10 HMRC manuals / legislation.gov.uk / gov.uk technical notes per bucket; the session selects 4-7 from the list to actually cite.
3. **Deepen §4.2 framing differentiator** from Stage 1's 1-2 sentence draft to 2-4 sentences (Wave 3 brief format).
4. **Flag cannibalisation risks** between Wave 4 siblings within and across buckets.
