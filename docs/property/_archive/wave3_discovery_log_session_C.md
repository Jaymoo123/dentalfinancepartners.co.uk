## D-7 | 2026-05-22 22:55Z | ADJACENT_TOPIC | C6 (PRS Database + Ombudsman) | s.74 Local Commissioners route is a discrete sub-topic worth a dedicated page

Section 74 of the RRA 2025 (the in-force-from-1-May-2026 part) extends the Local Government and Social Care Ombudsman jurisdiction to investigate complaints made by persons who are not tenants of the landlord (neighbours, occupiers of adjacent properties, other non-tenant complainants). It is a procedural extension of an existing route rather than the substantive Landlord Redress Scheme, and it has had almost no English-language press coverage because it sits outside the headline reform programme. A dedicated page on the s.74 route (who can complain, what the Local Commissioner does, what remedies are available, how it interacts with the substantive Ombudsman when commenced, what landlords should do if served with a s.74 complaint notice) would meet a real but small information need. Not in the Wave 3 pool. Flag for post-Wave-3 candidate review alongside D-3 (RRO mechanics) and D-5 (Decent Homes spend deductibility).
