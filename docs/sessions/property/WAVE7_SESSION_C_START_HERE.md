## CRITICAL drift catches inherited from Wave 7 HP-lock + §16.45 (13 catches; read before writing)

**Read all 13 in §16.45 of NETNEW_PROGRAM.** Operationally critical for Bucket C:

1. **TRS penalty £5,000 max DISCRETIONARY not graduated** — TRSM80020 case-by-case discretion; NOT the popular £100/£200/£300 tariff myth. C1 spotlight.
2. **CTA 2010 ss.464M-Q does NOT exist** — EOT bonus exemption is **ITEPA 2003 ss.312A-312I** (£3,600 per employer per tax year). C3 spotlight.
3. **EOT controlling-interest is "more than 50%" (50%+1)** under TCGA 1992 s.236M — equal-50/50 splits fail. Three-limb test: OSC + voting + profits + winding-up assets. C3 spotlight.
4. **EOT trading-company gating constraint** — property letting is NOT a trade (CG65700+); most landlord SPVs do NOT qualify for s.236H relief. Lead C3 with this gating.
5. **FA 2025 EOT reforms commenced 6 April 2025** — UK-resident trustee, independence test, consideration tightening, extended disqualifying-event period. C3 must use post-FA-2025 architecture.
6. **ITEPA s.312A £3,600 per employer per tax year** (NOT per employee). FA 2026 c. 11 uplift to £4,800 pending Royal Assent — verify operative figure at write time.
7. **FA 2003 s.108 (NOT Sch 4 para 5) is the linked-transactions definition.** Sch 4 para 5 is exchanges. s.108(1A) excludes Scottish + Welsh land. C5 spotlight.
8. **FA 2003 s.58C is "Relief for new zero-carbon homes"** (inserted by FA 2007), NOT cladding remediation. No SDLT cladding remediation relief exists in UK statute (post-Grenfell architecture uses VAT s.30A + Building Safety Levy + RPDT + BSA 2022 Sch 8 — none of which are SDLT relief). C6 was REPLACED with SDLT group relief depth (FA 2003 Sch 7).
9. **Sch 15 para 34 partnership share is income-profit-share** (NOT capital/voting). C9 spotlight.
10. **Sch 15 para 17A anti-withdrawal is 3 years** (NOT 7 years). C9 spotlight.
11. **HMRC SDLTM33500+ is the partnership-transfer manual** — NOT SDLTM09050+ which is s.75A Ramsay general anti-avoidance. C9 spotlight.
12. **Bewley narrowly applied post-Hyman/Mudan/MHB/Brown.** "In principle suitable" test; unmodernised "fixer-upper" remains residential. Bewley applies only to substantially structurally dangerous / contaminated / requiring complete reconstruction. C8 spotlight.
13. **SIPP/SSAS commercial-only — residential = taxable property under FA 2004 Sch 29A** (55-70% effective rate); commercial explicitly excluded (Sch 29A para 7).
