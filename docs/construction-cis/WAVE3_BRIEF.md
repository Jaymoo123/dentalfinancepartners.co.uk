**Mandatory pre-write rules for all wave-3 posts:**
- HP §3 CORRECTED position: ss.62A/62B penalty = **20% of the payment / 20% of sums returned**, on the **payer or return-maker**. NEVER "30% of tax lost on directors". Director liability is general officer-liability rules without a fixed percentage.
- Every GPS-related post must include the FA 2026 anti-fraud changes (immediate revocation, 5-year ban, "knew or should have known" standard, three due-diligence steps).
- AMAP mileage: **55p/mile** from 6 April 2026. Block 45p everywhere.
- Dividends: 10.75% / 35.75% / 39.35% from 6 April 2026. Block 8.75% / 33.75%.
- Employer NIC: **15% above £5,000** (not 13.8% / £9,100).
- No em-dashes anywhere. British English throughout.
- Refund = entry service, not rebate factory (HP §9, §12.B). Do not chase "DIY CIS refund" terms.
- Faceless brand: no self-ranking in "best of" lists; own "how-to-choose" and "cost/fee" angles instead (HP §12.A).
